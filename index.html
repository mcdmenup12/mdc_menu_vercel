Breakfast Hours at McDonald's UK

McDonald's breakfast menu is typically available every day from the restaurant opening time until 11:00 AM. Some locations may start serving breakfast as early as 5:00 AM, perfect for early risers or commuters.

Popular Items on the McDonald's Breakfast Menu UK

1. Egg McMuffin

The Egg McMuffin is a breakfast classic featuring a freshly toasted English muffin, a fluffy folded egg, a slice of melted cheese, and a slice of back bacon.

2. Sausage and Egg McMuffin

For meat lovers, the Sausage and Egg McMuffin replaces the bacon with a savory pork sausage patty, combined with egg and cheese on a toasted muffin.

3. Bacon Roll

A simple yet delicious choice, the Bacon Roll consists of crispy back bacon served in a soft white roll.

4. Breakfast Wraps

McDonald's UK also offers breakfast wraps filled with eggs, cheese, and your choice of bacon

5. Hotcakes and Syrup

For those with a sweet tooth, hotcakes served with syrup are a tasty morning treat.

Breakfast Combos and Deals

Many McDonald's UK locations offer breakfast combos that bundle a main item with a hot drink and a hash brown or another side.

Nutritional Information

McDonald's provides detailed nutritional information for all breakfast items. The Egg McMuffin contains about 300 calories and 18g of protein.
